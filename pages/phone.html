PHONE IS DEAD
Nearly every day a constant worry and complaint is that my phone is dying or dead. Is the battery on my phone malfunctioning? Or do I simply use my phone so much that it drains the charge extremely quickly?

To find out, I tracked the usage of my 9 most used apps. I discovered that each app uses a different amount of battery based on its use of data or internet, location services, notifications, pulled phone data, and camera use.

Click on each app to see the results.

On average, my phone is in use for 8.5 hours before it dies, most often from the heavy use of Messages, Snapchat, and looking at my phone's homescreen.
