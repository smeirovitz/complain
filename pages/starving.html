A common complaint among myself and college students everywhere is "I'm starving." Feeling hungry all the time is no fun feeling, especially when trying to avoid those inevitable extra pounds. But am I really not eating enough? Or is the stress of school and life promting me to want to constantly eat?

To find out, I used the My Fitness Pal App to track both my macro-nutrient and caloric intake for one week, not including the weekend.

Here are the results.

My suggested daily intake, based on my weight, height, and activity level, is 1,490 calories made up of 50% Carbs, 30% Fat, and 20% Protein.

My average daily intake is 1,378 calories, 57% Carbs, 34% Fat, and 9% Protein.
