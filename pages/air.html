THE AIR HURTS
Dealing with cold, Minnesota winters often make for a painful walk to class or work. But is the air really that much colder than normal? Is it really an abnormally cold winter?

To find out, I tracked the high temperatures during the week of January 5-11th and compared my findings with the average high tempteratures for that time of year.

Click on each day of the week to see the results.

On average, the high temperature for January in Minneapolis is 22°. The average I recorded was only 13°.
